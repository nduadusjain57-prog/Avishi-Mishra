One voice, three platforms

Each platform plays a different role — but they all come back to the same goal: making student life feel a little more honest and a little less overwhelming.

the long-form home

YouTube — study vlogs & college life

This is where the full story lives: study-with-me sessions, exam prep routines, hostel-life vlogs, and honest conversations about what it actually takes to stay consistent as a student. Long-form video allows the slower, more personal storytelling that short clips can't hold.

  • Study-with-me & productivity sessions
  • Day-in-the-life and college vlogs
  • Note-taking, planning & exam-prep guides
  • Honest, reflective "creator journey" videos
"welcome to your comfort place on the internet ♡" — channel tagline
the daily diary

Instagram — everyday moments & community

Instagram is the quicker, more personal layer of the brand — reels, photo dumps, and stories that capture the in-between moments a full video wouldn't cover. It's also the main space for direct back-and-forth with the community.

  • Reels recapping study sessions & routines
  • Photo dumps from campus and travel
  • Stories with polls, Q&As, and behind-the-scenes
  • Direct community engagement and DMs
Handle: @avishimishh
the professional side

LinkedIn — personal branding & the business of content

LinkedIn is where the conversation shifts from "student vlogger" to "young entrepreneur" — posts about the strategy behind building a creator career, lessons in personal branding, and the more professional side of turning content into a sustainable path.

  • Reflections on building a personal brand as a student
  • Lessons from managing content across platforms
  • Conversations with other young creators & founders
  • Professional milestones and collaborations
Bringing a student's perspective to the creator economy
the throughline

What ties it all together

Regardless of platform or format, every piece of content is built around the same four pillars.

📖

Education

Practical study systems, note-taking methods, and exam strategies that actually hold up under deadline pressure.

🌱

Productivity

Routines, planning frameworks, and habit-building content grounded in a real student schedule, not an idealised one.

🏡

College Life

The social, emotional, and logistical realities of hostel life, new cities, and growing up while still figuring things out.

✎

Creator Journey

Transparent, behind-the-scenes reflections on what it's actually like to build a personal brand from scratch.
