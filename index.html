the short version

A student who decided to document the process instead of hiding it

Avishi started sharing her study routines and college experiences because she couldn't find creators who talked about the messy, uncertain parts of student life — not just the aesthetic ones. What began as a way to stay accountable turned into a genuine community of students who show up for the same reason: to feel a little less alone in the process.
